Privacy Policy for Fokuss Chrome Extension

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Fokuss collects limited data necessary to provide its core functionality. This includes:

  • Information about your browsing activity on YouTube (such as tab URLs and watch time) to monitor and enforce daily watch time limits and filter video content based on user preferences.
  • Interaction data related to the extension’s use on YouTube pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Track and limit your daily YouTube watch time.
  • Filter YouTube video suggestions based on your specified keywords.
  • Provide a focused and controlled viewing experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties outside of what is required to provide the extension’s functionality.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Choices

You can adjust your settings within the extension to manage keywords and watch time limits.
